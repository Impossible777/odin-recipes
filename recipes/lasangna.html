Lasagna Recipe

Description

Are you ready for the best lasagna you've ever had? Well then, this is the recipe for you. This recipe utilizes a KitchenAid mixer to make fresh lasagna pasta, as well as fresh tomatoes to create your very own marinara sauce. Additionally, we will be adding in sausage, mozzarella and riccotta cheese to give it a wonderful flavor. Continue reading to see what ingredients are needed and how to prepare.

ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodle
  • mozzarella cheese
  • riccotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all your ingredients
  2. Step 2: Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat
  3. Step 3: Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with surgar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Step 4: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. Step 5: In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Step 6: Preaheat oven to 375 degrees F
  7. Step 7: To assemble, spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce on the bottom of baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread half of ricotta cheese micture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1.5 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  8. Step 8: Repeat the steps above until lasagna is the desired height. Cover the pain with tinfoil.
  9. Step 9: Bake in preheated over for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 miniutes.
