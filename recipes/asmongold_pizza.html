Asmongold's Infamous Pizza Recipe

Description

Are you ready for the best Totino's pizza you've ever had? Well then, this is the recipe for you. We are going to use mozzarella and pepperonis to take this mediocre, but fantastic store bought pizza to the next level.

ingredients

  • Totino's Pepperoni Pizza
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Sliced Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all your ingredients
  2. Step 2: Preheat oven to the temperature recommended on the package
  3. Step 3: Open totino's pizza and place it a cookie sheet with non-stick paper
  4. Step 4: Cover pizza with mozzarella cheese
  5. Step 5: Add 4 pepperonis horizontally across the pizza
  6. Step 6: Repeat step 5 for 3 rows
  7. Step 7: Cook pizza for the recommended amount of time
  8. Step 8: Enjoy.
